Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting us, you might find answers to common questions below:

How accurate is the pregnancy due date calculator?

Our calculator uses the standard Naegele's Rule (280 days from LMP) which provides a reliable estimate. However, actual delivery dates can vary. Always consult with your healthcare provider for medical advice.

Do you store my pregnancy data?

No. Our calculator processes your Last Menstrual Period date locally in your browser. We do not store or transmit this information to our servers. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

Can I use this calculator if I have irregular periods?

Yes, but the calculation assumes a regular 28-day cycle. If you have irregular periods, the estimate may be less accurate. Your healthcare provider can provide a more precise due date using ultrasound measurements.

Is this website free to use?

Yes, all our tools and resources are completely free. We do not require registration or payment to use our pregnancy due date calculator or access our information.

Can I suggest new features or content?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for improving our website. Please use the contact form above or email us at editor@pregnancydate.online with your ideas.
