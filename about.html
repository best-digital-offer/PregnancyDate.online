About PregnancyDate.online

Your trusted resource for accurate pregnancy due date calculations and reliable pregnancy information.

Our Mission

At PregnancyDate.online, our mission is to provide expectant parents with accurate, accessible, and reliable pregnancy information and tools. We understand that pregnancy is a special journey filled with questions and anticipation, and we're here to help you navigate it with confidence.

Founded with the goal of making pregnancy information more accessible, our platform offers a simple yet powerful pregnancy due date calculator based on the medically accepted Naegele's Rule, along with comprehensive week-by-week pregnancy guides and resources.

What We Offer

Accurate Due Date Calculator

Our primary tool is the pregnancy due date calculator, which uses your Last Menstrual Period (LMP) to estimate your expected delivery date. This calculation follows the standard 280-day (40-week) pregnancy model used by healthcare professionals worldwide.

Comprehensive Pregnancy Information

Beyond due date calculation, we provide detailed information about:

  • Pregnancy week-by-week development
  • Trimesters and key milestones
  • Common symptoms and changes
  • Preparation for labor and delivery
  • Postpartum recovery information

Accessible Resources

All our tools and information are completely free to use, with no registration required. We believe that reliable pregnancy information should be available to everyone, regardless of their circumstances.

Our Values

Accuracy

We prioritize medically accurate information and clearly distinguish between established facts and general advice.

Accessibility

Our tools and resources are designed to be easy to use and understand for everyone, regardless of technical expertise.

Privacy

We respect your privacy and do not store personal pregnancy data. All calculations happen locally in your browser.

Transparency

We're clear about the limitations of our tools and always recommend consulting healthcare professionals for medical advice.

Editorial Standards

We maintain high editorial standards to ensure the information we provide is reliable and up-to-date:

  • Content is reviewed regularly and updated based on current medical guidelines
  • We cite reputable medical sources and research
  • We clearly distinguish between evidence-based information and general advice
  • We acknowledge the limitations of online tools and calculators

While we strive for accuracy, we always emphasize that our tools provide estimates and information, not medical diagnoses or treatment recommendations. We encourage users to consult with healthcare providers for personalized medical advice.

Content Updates

Our content is reviewed and updated regularly to reflect current medical understanding and best practices. If you notice information that needs correction or updating, please contact us at editor@pregnancydate.online.
